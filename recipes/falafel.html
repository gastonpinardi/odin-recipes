Falafel

Falafel are crisp and delicious deep-fried legume fritters made with beans, spices, onions, and herbs.

Ingredients

  • Dry Chickpeas
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Oil
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Put the Dry Chickpeas in water and let them soak for 12 hours.
  2. Throw away the soaking water, and put the hydrated beans with the onion, garlic, cumin, salt and pepper in a blender.
  3. Make uniform balls with the mix, and fry them in hot oil.
