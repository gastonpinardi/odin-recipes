Hummus

Hummus is a Middle Eastern dip, spread, or savory dish made from cooked, mashed chickpeas blended with tahini, lemon juice, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • Dry Chickpeas
  • Tahini
  • Lemon Juice
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • Pepper
  • Cold Water

Steps

  1. Let the chickpeas hydrate for 12 hours in water.
  2. Once hydrated, let them cook them in boiling water.
  3. Put all the ingredients in a blender and blend until getting a homogeneous cream.
